Income 35, Child Care Contribution Credit

Donations to Seeds of Learning may qualify for a 50% Colorado tax credit

Donations to Seeds of Learning may qualify for a 50% Colorado tax credit 

To qualify you must have a Colorado tax liability equal to or greater than the credit. In addition, if you itemize deductions on your Federal tax return, there is another tax benefit.

Learn More

For example, for those in the 25% Federal tax bracket, a $1,000 donation would result in a $750 savings- $500 on Colorado taxes and $250 on Federal taxes. This Colorado tax law is a win-win for those who support and who provide early education. Seeds of Learning uses generous donations to continue to educate at-risk children and care for toddlers, while donors like you are rewarded with an excellent tax break.

Be sure to give your tax preparer the tax credit certification form DR 1317, furnished by Seeds of Learning at year-end, as evidence of your donation.

Example: Gift of $5,000
(illustration 35% Federal tax bracket)*
*Situations vary; consult your tax advisor.

Colorado State Income Tax Savings : $232

Colorado Income Tax Credit: $2,500

Federal Income Tax Savings*: $794

Reduced Federal and Colorado Income Tax: $3,526

After-tax Cost of giving $5,000: $1,474

 

NOTE: Because of the difficult economic times, certain conditions must be met each year to take advantage of the credit.  Please check with your tax advisor for the most current information.
